Bindubasini Football Championship Website

Bindubasini Govt. Boys’ High School, commonly referred to as Bindubasini Boys or B. B. Boys’, is a public boys' high school in Tangail, Bangladesh. The school was established in 3rd April 1880, patronized and endowed by Rani Bindubasini Chowdhurany and named after her as Bindubasini Model High School. It has a glorious history of 143 years of education, social work and athletes. In 1996, Bindubasini Govt. Boys’ High School, Tangail achieved the status of best high school in Bangladesh. In 1996 it was awarded best Institution from Ministry of Education. It achieved 8th place in Dhaka Board in the SSC examination in 2008. 5th place in 2011, it achieved 1st place among government high schools in Bangladesh in 2015, it achieved 5th Place in Dhaka Board. It was placed 1 st amongst the government schools of Bangladesh in the year of 2011, 2015, 2016 and 2017. In 2015, this school was selected as the best government high school of Bangladesh. In 2016 this School was selected as the best Institution in Mymensingh Region. Bindubashini Govt. Boys’ High School Has achieved some brilliant Success in Sports. In 1994-1995 this school became Champion in 13th Nirman School Cricket and in 1998 -99 this school again became Champion in 17th National School Cricket. In Sub Camp Running Shield Binsubashini Scout Den have achieved 2nd position in 2014 and 3rd position in 2010. Former chief justice and former acting president of Bangladesh Government Abu Syed Chowdhury, novelist Manik Bandyopadhyay, former director General of RAB and IG of Bangladesh Police Hasan Mahmud Khandaker and such type of honorable persons were studied here and became successful in their relevant field of work. Bindubasini Govt. Boys’ High School has a history in both cricket and football. In 1996, this school won the Inter school football Championship trophy among all schools of Bangladesh. In 2010 the junior football team crossed Mymensingh division. In inter district football premier league, our school becomes champion almost every year.
